📜 Welcome to the Emporium

Welcome, adventurer! Before you browse our wares, take a moment to familiarise yourself with how trade works in the realm. Gold doesn't grow on trees — unless you're a Druid, in which case, please put it back.

Purchasing items is simple: find what you need, note the cost, and hand over your coin to the Dungeon Master at the start of your session. Your DM will deduct the amount from your character's gold total.

☯ Currency & Exchange Rates

Three coins are in common use across the realm. The gold piece is the standard of wealth — one gp can buy a bedroll, 50 feet of rope, or feed a goat for a month.

📦 Adventurer Kits

When creating your character you can choose a class equipment package that includes one of these packs. If you are buying your own starting equipment you can also purchase a pack outright — usually cheaper than buying items individually.

Packs are topped up at the start of each session — you never need to track individual rations, torches, or candles.
